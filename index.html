About Xephortia

The Xephortian Republic is a landlocked micronation based in the British Isles, founded on 8 November 2025. It uses a Councilative Democratic system led by a Taoiseach.

Built for elections

The portal allows approved citizens to sign in, cast a single vote, and view results that are automatically counted by the system without manual vote tallying.

Moeezland support

Moeezland and Xephortia share strong diplomatic relations. This portal was provided as a technical contribution to help strengthen trust in elections.

How this portal protects the vote

Verified voter access

Only approved citizen email addresses are allowed to access the voting portal.

One vote per citizen

Each voter receives one fixed vote record per election, helping prevent duplicate voting.

Automatic counting

Results are calculated directly from recorded votes, reducing manual interference and errors.

Public result access

Past election results can be viewed separately without forcing visitors directly into login.

About the Xephortian Republic

Xephortia's capital is Memeburg. Its government includes a Board of Ministers, a National Council, and a Regional Council. The republic describes its foreign policy as neutral and friendly, aiming for strong relations with other micronations.
